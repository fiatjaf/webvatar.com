Webvatar

A usable avatar, fetched directly from the target person's website, which should be their identity, you can embed at your site easily (for example, when you let people comment or login with their domain, if you're scraping or republishing something from their website) with <img src="http://webvatar.com/TARGET-DOMAIN">.

How does it work?

It searches for .u-photo marked images or links in the target webpage, according to the microformats2 specification, falls back on [rel="icon"] and, if nothing was found (or if the found image is too small and the request was made with the acceptsmall=n querystring parameter), it returns an image with the domain name written in, or some other image, such as a RoboHash or some of those Gravatar default images for each domain, according to what you indicate in the alt= querystring parameter.
